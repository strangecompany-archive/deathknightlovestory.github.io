Writer / Director

Hugh Hancock

Hugh Hancock has pioneered real-time 3D animation for more than 15 years. He founded Machinima.com, now the fourth biggest YouTube channel in the world. He directed the Creative Commons-licensed "Punk Fantasy" film "BloodSpell", wrote the book "Machinima for Dummies", and has been quoted on Machinima and animation in the New York Times, the BBC, and the Guardian.
