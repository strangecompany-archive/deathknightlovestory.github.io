How Death Knight Love Story Was Made

Death Knight Love Story's production combined techniques from Machinima (game-based filmmaking), motion capture (a la Gollum and Avatar) and historical research on Medieval martial arts to create a unique animated film.

We'll add more articles on the making of DKLS to this page over time, so do check back later!
